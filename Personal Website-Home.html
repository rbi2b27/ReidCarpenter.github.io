About Me


I am originally from a small town in rural Northern California near the Napa Valley.
I relocated to Marina, California in fall of 2013 to attend California State University Monterey Bay.
I have studied Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing for the past four
and will be graduating in May of 2017. During my time at school I have also held
a job as a Front Desk Agent at a hotel. Monterey and its surrounding area could not have been a more
perfect location to attend school.

In my down time I enjoy traveling, going outdoors, listening and playing music,
and watching sports. It has been very easy for me to enjoy all of these activities
while living in Monterey. Check out my Hobbies and Interests
for more on what I do in my off time.
