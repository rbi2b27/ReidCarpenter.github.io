Educational Background


Growing up in a small town, I did not have many options when it came to education.
I attended Middletown High School in Middletown, California. There I participated
in many clubs including yearbook and choir and played football and baseball.
I graduated with high honors and was able to choose between a few different colleges
that I was accepted to. I ended up choosing California State University, Monterey Bay as
it seemed to have an up and comming business program, as well as a beautiful sorrounding area.











I began attending California State Unversity, Monterey Bay in the Fall of 2013. Though I did not know exactly what I wanted to do with my career at that point, I enrolled into the College of Business. During my first few semesters I became interested in Economics and Finance, however, there was not a major or concentration for either of those fields. Trying to relate closely to my interest in Economics and Finance, I decided to declare my concentration in Accounting. After a couple semesters of course work I had decided that this was not the path that I wanted to continue on for the rest of my career and switched my concentration to Marketing.

During my time as a student in the College of Business, I gained in various of the business world including accounting, finance, economics, management, international business, entrepreneurship and, of course, marketing. I also had the opportunity to take a few classes outside of the core classes of the business program which included an eccommerce website design class, a hiking class, and a kayaking class.
